Welcome to The Virtual Brain documentation!
===================================

.. toctree::
   :maxdepth: 2
   
The Virtual Brain (TVB) is a framework for analyzing and modeling neuroimaging data.

To download it, check out the `official TVB website <http://thevirtualbrain.org/app/index.html>`_

For installation, refer to :ref:`installation_manual`

After it's installed, see the web interface's user guide, :ref:`user_guide`
or, alternatively, the tutorials on using the console interface :ref:`tutorials`

Lastly, when importing your own data, you may find :ref:`a_description_of_a_complete_dataset`
useful.


To get in touch with other users and developers, please head over to the
`mailing list <https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/tvb-users>`_.
